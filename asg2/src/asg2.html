Notes for Graders

Click/drag to move the camera and scroll to zoom.

Shift+click to make the cat's pupils dilate.

The upper portions of the cat's tail is a third-level joint.

The cat's ears are triangular prisms (Non-cube primitive).

The only slider that has an effect while the animation is running is the "Tail Base Angle" slider.

Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
